SSH Tunnel (for OpenClaw Dashboard)

OpenClaw's dashboard requires HTTPS. Run this on your computer:

ssh -L 18790:localhost:18790 picocluster@picoclaw

Then open https://localhost:18790 — token: picocluster-token
